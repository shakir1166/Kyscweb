About KYSC

Kotegoda Youth Sports Club (KYSC) is dedicated to fostering sportsmanship, teamwork, and fitness within our community. Through quality training and support, we empower young athletes to excel in sports and life.

Kickboxing

Our popular kickboxing program builds strength, coordination, and confidence for all levels.

Soccer

Join our soccer teams to learn skills, teamwork, and enjoy competitive play.

Cricket

Our cricket coaching helps players of all ages master the game and enjoy team spirit.

Volleyball

Play volleyball, enhance fitness, and build camaraderie in a supportive environment.
